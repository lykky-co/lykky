# Oulu GTM Resource Directory

Oulu punches above its weight. A city of 220,000 with a 6G research program, a European Capital of Culture year, and an ecosystem built on decades of Nokia R&D alumni. If you are building a startup here, you have access to infrastructure that most European cities cannot match.

This is a no-fluff directory of the organizations, programs, funding sources, and communities that actually matter for your go-to-market in 2026. Every link has been verified. Every program described is running or accepting applications.

---

## Your First Stops

These are the organizations you contact in your first week. They are free, they are used to working with early-stage founders, and they will connect you to everything else on this list.

### BusinessOulu

The city's business development company. Free advisory services for starting, growing, and internationalizing your business. They run startup programs, connect you to funding, and maintain relationships with every other organization listed here.

- **What they do:** Business advisory, startup programs, internationalization support, investor connections
- **Cost:** Free
- **Where:** Uusikatu 52 (BusinessAsema), Oulu
- **Programs:** Startup Express (pre-incubator, runs multiple cohorts per year)
- **Link:** [businessoulu.com/en](https://www.businessoulu.com/en/)

### StartupOulu

The startup-specific arm of Oulu's ecosystem. Runs events, programs, and the Startup Station co-working space. Their newsletter is the single best way to stay informed about what is happening in the Oulu startup scene.

- **What they do:** Startup programs, events, community, newsletter
- **Cost:** Free
- **Link:** [startupoulu.com](https://www.startupoulu.com/)

### Business Finland (Oulu Office)

The national innovation funding agency. They offer grants (not equity) for R&D, internationalization, and market validation. If your startup has any technology component, you should be talking to Business Finland.

- **What they do:** Non-dilutive grants, internationalization programs, global network
- **Key grants:** Sprint (up to 100k, replaces Tempo/NIY in 2026), Deep Tech Accelerator (up to 1.2M for research-based startups), Research to Business (for commercializing research)
- **Cost:** Free to apply
- **Link:** [businessfinland.fi](https://www.businessfinland.com/)

## Programs and Accelerators

### Startup Express

The pre-incubator program that turns ideas into startups in 100 days. Run by BusinessOulu, it includes weekly workshops, mentoring, and community support. The training is available in both Finnish and English. Multiple cohorts run per year — the spring 2026 cohort started January 22.

- **Duration:** ~100 days
- **Cost:** Free
- **Where:** Startup Station at BusinessAsema
- **Who it is for:** Anyone with a business idea or wanting to join an existing team
- **Link:** [Startup Express Pre-incubator](https://oulu.com/en/startup-express-pre-incubator/)

### Startup Weekend Oulu

A 54-hour event where you pitch an idea on Friday, build a team, validate the concept, and present on Sunday. Good for testing ideas, finding co-founders, and getting into the community fast.

- **Frequency:** Multiple times per year
- **Cost:** Ticket-based (affordable)
- **Link:** [startupweekendoulu.com](https://www.startupweekendoulu.com/)

### OYSTER Incubator (Health Tech)

A digital health R&D and innovation center opened in 2024 at Oulu University Hospital. If you are building anything in health tech, this is where you validate it. Offers multidisciplinary collaboration, public-private partnerships, and direct access to clinical environments.

- **Focus:** Digital health, medtech
- **Link:** [ouluhealth.fi/oyster](https://ouluhealth.fi/oyster/)

## Funding

### Important: Business Finland 2026 Changes

Business Finland restructured its startup funding for 2026. **Tempo and NIY (Young Innovative Companies) grants have been closed** — they will not reopen. The government's 2026 budget reduced non-R&D funding authorizations by 27M compared to 2025. The strategic shift is toward higher-ambition R&D funding. Here is what replaced them:

Open March 2 — August 31, 2026. | [Sprint Grant](https://www.businessfinland.fi/en/services/funding/calls/2026/sprint-grant-funding-call/) |

### Outside Oulu City: Leader Groups and Rural Funding

If your company is located outside Oulu city — in Ii, Pudasjärvi, Muhos, Kuusamo, Ylivieska, or other municipalities in Pohjois-Pohjanmaa — you have access to EU rural development funding through Leader groups. This is a significant and often overlooked funding source for micro-enterprises (up to 5 employees).

| Leader Group | Area | Link |
|-------------|------|------|
| Oulun Seudun Leader | Ii, Pudasjärvi, Utajärvi, Muhos, Kempele, rural Oulu areas | [maaseutu.fi](https://maaseutu.fi/pohjois-suomi/pohjois-pohjanmaa/hanke-ja-yritystuet/) |
| Koillismaan Leader | Kuusamo, Taivalkoski, Posio | [maaseutu.fi](https://maaseutu.fi/pohjois-suomi/pohjois-pohjanmaa/hanke-ja-yritystuet/) |
| Rieska-Leader | Kalajoki, Ylivieska, Oulainen, Sievi, and more | [maaseutu.fi](https://maaseutu.fi/pohjois-suomi/pohjois-pohjanmaa/hanke-ja-yritystuet/) |
| Keskipiste-Leader | Nivala, Haapajärvi, Haapavesi, Siikalatva, and more | [keskipisteleader.fi](https://keskipisteleader.fi/yritykset/leader-yritystuet/) |

Applications are processed continuously through the Hyrrä service portal. Focus areas include agriculture and food value chains, tourism, and nature-based services, but also general micro-enterprise support.

**Pohjois-Pohjanmaan liitto** (the regional council) coordinates EU structural funding (EAKR/ESR+) for the region and runs AKKE project funding. They cannot fund individual companies directly, but they fund development projects that benefit startups — worth watching for ecosystem-level opportunities.

- **Link:** [pohjois-pohjanmaa.fi](https://www.pohjois-pohjanmaa.fi/en/)
- **Funding calls:** [rakennerahastot.fi](https://rakennerahastot.fi/rakennerahastot.fi/pohjois-suomi/rahoituksen-hakeminen/pohjois-pohjanmaan-rahoitushaut)

### Other Funding Paths

- **Finnvera** — Government-backed loans and guarantees for startups. Not a grant, but often the easiest debt financing available. [finnvera.fi](https://www.finnvera.fi/eng)
- **EIC Accelerator** — EU program, up to 2.5M grant + 15M equity. Competitive but Oulu startups have a track record here. Oulu won the European Capital of Innovation Award (iCapital) in 2024-25.
- **Nordic Option** — Seed-stage fund focused on Finnish startups. [nordicoption.fi](https://nordicoption.fi/en/)

## Workspaces and Labs

### Business Corner

OAMK leads the development of student-led startup activities and offers a wide variety of support for future startup entrepreneurs. The goal of Business Corner activities is to foster the growth of new startup and growth companies. They do this with a startup program, numerous events, and peer support. Everyone interested in startups and entrepreneurship is welcome to come work together in Co-work.

- **Where:** Yliopistokatu 9, entrance 5C, Oulu
- **Link:** [oamk.fi/en/development-platforms/business-corner](https://www.oamk.fi/en/development-platforms/business-corner)

### BusinessAsema

The main startup hub in central Oulu. Houses BusinessOulu services, Startup Station, the Enterprise Agency, and FabLab (prototyping lab with manufacturing equipment). Walk-in mentoring available Thursdays 9:30-11:30.

- **Where:** Uusikatu 52, Oulu
- **Link:** [businessasema.com](https://businessasema.com/en/)

### Tellus Innovation Arena

University of Oulu's open innovation space at Linnanmaa campus. Free co-working for university-affiliated people. Six meeting rooms, stage areas, and event space. Also present at Kontinkangas campus. Walk-in startup mentoring Wednesdays 9:30-11:30.

- **Where:** Erkki Koiso-Kanttilan katu 1, Linnanmaa campus
- **Cost:** Free for university staff and students
- **Link:** [oulu.fi/en/cooperation/tellus](https://www.oulu.fi/en/cooperation/tellus)

### OuluHealth Labs

World-class health tech testing environments across the service chain — hospital, health centers, and home environments. Consists of OYS TestLab (hospital testing at Oulu University Hospital), OAMK SimLab (simulation environments), and home-based testing.

- **Focus:** Health and medtech validation
- **Link:** [ouluhealth.fi](https://ouluhealth.fi/)

## Research and Technology Partnerships

### 6G Flagship

The world's first 6G research program, running at University of Oulu since 2018 with a 251M budget. If your product involves wireless, IoT, connectivity, or edge computing, this is a globally unique asset sitting in your backyard. The program runs through mid-2026, with 6G standardization expected to be in full swing by then.

- **Research areas:** Wireless connections, distributed intelligence, human-centric services
- **Verticals:** Health, industry, vehicles, energy
- **Link:** [6gflagship.com](https://www.6gflagship.com/)

### Oulu Innovation Alliance (OIA)

Strategic consortium of the University of Oulu, OAMK, VTT, the city, and industry partners. Coordinates ecosystem-level initiatives and maintains a database of 140+ Oulu startups. Their spearhead programs cover startup entrepreneurship, health, printed intelligence, circular economy, and automotive.

- **Startup database:** [oulu.com/en/oia/results/database-of-oulu-startups](https://oulu.com/en/oia/results/database-of-oulu-startups/)
- **Link:** [oulu.com/en/oia](https://oulu.com/en/oia/)

### University of Oulu

Strong research partnerships available in wireless/6G, AI, health tech, and industrial applications. Thesis workers and interns are a practical way to get R&D capacity at low cost while building relationships with the research community.

- **Link:** [oulu.fi](https://www.oulu.fi/en/)

### OAMK (Oulu University of Applied Sciences)

Practical talent pipeline for business, IT, and engineering. Good source of interns and project workers, especially for business development and marketing tasks relevant to GTM execution.

- **Link:** [oamk.fi](https://www.oamk.fi/en/)

## Community and Events

### Polar Bear Pitching

Oulu's signature startup event — founders pitch from an ice hole in the frozen sea. In 2026, the event is exclusively open to the public as part of Oulu2026 European Capital of Culture. The day conference covers "Cultural Capital in Startup Growth" with workshops, matchmaking, and networking. Even if you do not pitch, attend. This is where the ecosystem meets.

- **When:** February (annual)
- **Where:** Tori Market Square, Oulu
- **Link:** [polarbearpitching.com](https://polarbearpitching.com/)

### Oulu Entrepreneurship Society (OuluES)

Student entrepreneurship community founded in 2010, based at Tellus Innovation Arena. Organizes Startup Sprint (24-hour events), workshops, and peer mentoring. Good for finding technical co-founders and early team members from the university.

- **Link:** [oulues.com](https://www.oulues.com/)

### Trusted Cofounder

The official startup talent pool platform for StartupOulu. Connects Northern Finland's builders to a nationwide network. Use this if you are looking for co-founders or early team members.

- **Link:** [trustedcofounder.com](https://www.trustedcofounder.com/)

## Going International from Oulu

### Nordic Innovation House

Soft-landing programs for Nordic startups expanding to the US, Singapore, Hong Kong, and Tokyo. The TINC accelerator is a 6-week program for US market validation. The REACH program is a 6-month incubator for research-based tech with two 14-day Silicon Valley visits.

- **Locations:** Silicon Valley, New York, Singapore, Hong Kong, Tokyo
- **Link:** [nordicinnovationhouse.com](https://www.nordicinnovationhouse.com/)

### Team Finland

Coordinated internationalization support across Business Finland, Finnvera, Finnish embassies, and trade offices. Single point of access to Finland's international business support network.

- **Link:** [team.finland.fi](https://www.team.finland.fi/en)

## Practical First Steps Checklist

If you are starting a company in Oulu today, do these in order:

1. **Register your company** at [ytj.fi](https://www.ytj.fi/en/) and set up at [suomi.fi](https://www.suomi.fi/company)
2. **Book a free advisory session** at [BusinessOulu](https://www.businessoulu.com/en/businesses-and-entrepreneurs/starting-a-business/)
3. **Subscribe** to the [StartupOulu newsletter](https://newsletter.startupoulu.com/)
4. **Apply to Startup Express** if you are pre-product — [startupoulu.com](https://www.startupoulu.com/services/getting-started.html)
5. **Sign up** at Polar Bear Pitching, OuES events, and Startup Station drop-in mentoring
6. **Join Trusted Cofounder** if you need team members — [trustedcofounder.com](https://www.trustedcofounder.com/)

The single most important thing you can do is show up consistently. The community is welcoming, but it rewards people who are visibly building.
