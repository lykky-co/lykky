## First Steps Checklist

1. Register at [ytj.fi](https://www.ytj.fi/en/), set up at [suomi.fi](https://www.suomi.fi/company)
2. Book free advisory at [BusinessOulu](https://www.businessoulu.com/en/businesses-and-entrepreneurs/starting-a-business/)
3. Subscribe to [StartupOulu newsletter](https://newsletter.startupoulu.com/)
4. Apply to [Startup Express](https://www.startupoulu.com/services/getting-started.html) if pre-product
5. Attend Polar Bear Pitching, OuluES events, Startup Station drop-in mentoring
6. Join [Trusted Cofounder](https://www.trustedcofounder.com/) if you need a team
